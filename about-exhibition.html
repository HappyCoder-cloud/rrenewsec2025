    About Exhibition

    RENEWSEC 2025 will provide a platform to OT Cybersecurity Solutions Providers, Technology Providers, Automation & AI, Cyber Insurance Providers & Other Service Providers in Renewable Energy Sector to come under one roof to showcase their latest technologies, Solutions & Services.

    This unique platform will allow key stakeholders—including Renewable Energy Operators, (Solar, Wind, Hydro & Green Hydrogen etc), State Utilities and Regulators—to explore the latest technologies and best practices for protecting critical energy assets from emerging cyber threats.
