About RENEWSEC 2025

RENEWSEC 2025 is India's premier event for OT cybersecurity in the Renewable & Clean Energy sector, bringing together CISOs, technology leaders, regulators, DISCOMs, plant operators, utility companies and innovators to secure the nation’s green future.

With India's focus on scaling up solar, wind, hydro, hydrogen and storage, robust OT cybersecurity is key for uptime, safety, compliance and national security.

Why OT Cybersecurity in the RE Sector is Important?

  • Protecting critical infrastructure and grid reliability
  • Securing nationwide grid dependency and interconnectivity
  • Defending against rising cyber threats and complex attacks
  • Addressing vulnerabilities in legacy/digitized systems
  • Preventing environmental and safety risks
  • Complying with regulatory and reporting protocols
  • Mitigating supplier and third-party security risks
  • Powering India's net-zero, future-ready grid
